KindPoints

A child-friendly reward board app that reinforces positive behavior through structured rewards and immediate feedback.

Designed for children ages 3-12 • Built with ABA principles

✨ Core Features

⭐

Behavior Tracking Dashboard

Instant visual feedback with "Good" and "Oops" buttons. Track progress in real-time with a prominent token count display and scrollable history log.

🎯

Goal Setting & Progress

Create configurable reward goals with optional images. Visual progress bars track achievement, and redeem stars when goals are reached.

🎨

Customizable Settings

Adjust sound volumes independently, select audio output devices, choose color schemes (Light/Dark/System), and reset all data with one tap.

🔒

Privacy-First Design

All data stored locally on your device. No cloud sync, no third-party SDKs, and iOS encryption for complete user control over their child's data.

🔒 Our Privacy Commitment

KindPoints is built with privacy-by-default. We are committed to protecting your child's privacy and ensuring the security of their personal information.

✅ LOCAL-ONLY DATA STORAGE All user data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers. Your progress, settings, and preferences remain entirely on your device.
✅ NO PERSONAL INFORMATION COLLECTED We do NOT collect account information, email addresses, location data, health records, or financial information. The app requires no user accounts or authentication.
✅ NO THIRD-PARTY SDKS No analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs are integrated. We comply with Apple's strict App Store privacy requirements and have been reviewed for data collection practices.

What Data Is Stored Locally:

  • • Behavior tracking data (points earned, goals achieved, streaks)
  • • Goal information (titles, star counts, images)
  • • Settings preferences (sound volumes, color scheme, audio route)
  • • Usage statistics (stored locally only)

Your Rights:

Under GDPR & CCPA, you have the right to:

  • • Access all personal data we hold about you
  • • Request deletion of your data
  • • Opt-out of data sharing (we don't share any data)
🔐 Simplest Method: Use the "Reset All Data" button in Settings to immediately delete all your data.
