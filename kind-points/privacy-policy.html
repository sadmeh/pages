📜 Privacy Policy for KindPoints App

Last Updated: April 25, 2026 | Version 1.0

🔒 Introduction

KindPoints is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our KindPoints mobile application (the "App").

🔒 PRIVACY-BY-DEFAULT APPROACH The KindPoints App is designed with privacy-by-default. All user data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers.

📝 Information We Collect

Personal Information Provided by You

When you use the KindPoints App, we collect only the following types of information strictly for app functionality:

  • Behavior Tracking Data: Points earned, goals achieved, streaks completed, daily progress tracking
  • Goal Information: Goal titles, required star counts, goal images (if attached)
  • Settings Preferences: Customizations you make within the app (sound volumes, color scheme, audio route)
❌ WE DO NOT COLLECT: Account information (no user accounts or email addresses required), Personal identification information (name, email, phone number), Location data, Health records or medical information, Financial information.

Automatically Collected Information

The App may automatically collect the following only for app functionality:

  • Usage Statistics: How you interact with the App (features used, time spent) - stored locally only
  • Local Storage Data: Progress data, settings, and preferences stored on your device
  • Device Information: Device type and operating system version (required by iOS for app compatibility)
🔐 IMPORTANT NOTE: We do NOT collect sensitive personal information such as health records, location data, or financial information.

🎯 How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  1. App Functionality: To provide and maintain the core features of the App (behavior tracking, goal management)
  2. Personalization: To customize your experience based on your preferences (sound volumes, color scheme)
  3. Progress Tracking: To display your achievements, streaks, and goal completion rates
  4. Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve app performance (all data remains local)
❌ WE DO NOT USE YOUR DATA FOR: Marketing or advertising purposes, Third-party sharing, Selling user information, Creating user profiles.

💾 Data Storage & Security

Where Your Data Is Stored

All of your data is stored locally on your device. We do NOT store any user data on our servers or third-party cloud services. This means:

  • ✅ Your progress is saved to your device using SwiftData
  • ✅ Your settings and preferences are local only
  • ✅ No data leaves your device without your consent
  • ✅ iOS provides built-in encryption for app data at rest
  • ✅ Data is stored in sandboxed areas of your device

Security Measures

We implement the following security practices:

  • 🔒 Local Storage: Data is stored in secure, sandboxed areas of your device using SwiftData
  • 🔒 Encryption: iOS provides built-in encryption for app data at rest
  • 🔒 No Server-Side Storage: Eliminates risk of server-side breaches
  • 🔒 App Store Guidelines: We comply with Apple's strict App Store privacy requirements
  • 🔒 No Third-Party SDKs: No analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs integrated

Data Retention

Your data remains on your device until you:

  • Delete the app
  • Clear app data in settings (via "Reset All Data" button)
  • Uninstall the app
🗑️ WE DO NOT RETAIN ANY DATA AFTER YOU DELETE THE APP.

🔄 Data Sharing

We do NOT share, sell, or trade your personal information with third parties.

❌ OUR COMMITMENT TO PRIVACY MEANS: No selling of user data, No sharing with advertisers or marketing partners, No analytics services that track users, No third-party integrations that collect data, No cloud synchronization.

Exception: We may share anonymized, aggregated usage statistics with Apple for App Store review purposes only.

👶 Children's Privacy

KindPoints is designed for children ages 3-12 and their caregivers. We are committed to protecting children's privacy:

  • No Personal Information Collection: The app does not require or collect personal information from children
  • Parental Control: Caregivers can manage all data through the Settings menu
  • Data Deletion: All data can be deleted at any time by removing the app or using "Reset All Data"
  • 🔒 Age Appropriateness: Content and features are designed for young children with simple, intuitive interfaces
⚠️ IMPORTANT NOTE: KindPoints is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child under 13 without verification of parental consent, we will take steps to delete that data.

📝 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

🛡️ Consent & Opt-Out Mechanisms

Our app implements privacy-by-design principles:

  • ✅ Minimal Data Collection: We only collect data essential for app functionality (behavior tracking, goals)
  • ✅ Clear Purpose: Users understand exactly why their data is used (progress tracking, goal achievement)
  • ✅ Easy Deletion: Users can delete all data by removing the app or using "Reset All Data" in Settings
  • ✅ No Pre-Checked Consent: No pre-selected options for data sharing or analytics

⚠️ Dark Pattern Avoidance

We commit to NOT using dark patterns:

  • ❌ No confusing privacy settings
  • ❌ No pre-checked consent boxes
  • ❌ No misleading "accept all" buttons
  • ❌ No making privacy choices harder than accepting data collection

🔍 Data Audit & Transparency

We have conducted a comprehensive data audit and mapped all data touchpoints in our app:

  • ✅ Data Inventory: Complete inventory of all personal data collected (behavior events, goals, settings)
  • ✅ Purpose Mapping: Clear documentation of why each data point is collected (progress tracking)
  • ✅ Third-Party Review: No third-party SDKs or analytics services integrated
  • ✅ Local Storage Verification: Confirmed all data remains on device (no cloud sync, no external servers)
  • ✅ No Account System: No user accounts, email collection, or authentication required
